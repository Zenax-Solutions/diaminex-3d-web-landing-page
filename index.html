ABOUT US

At Diaminex, we specialize in bringing the finest gems from Sri Lanka, a country renowned for its rich heritage in producing some of the world's most exquisite gems. With centuries of expertise in gem mining, Sri Lanka is known for its rare and high-quality gems that captivate with their brilliance and beauty.

Our mission is to offer these treasures to discerning clients around the globe, providing unparalleled quality, authenticity, and craftsmanship. Whether you're a collector, jeweler, or enthusiast, we are committed to delivering exceptional gems with a promise of excellence, integrity, and trust.

OUR COLLECTION

Ceylon Sapphire

"Renowned for their exceptional color saturation and clarity"

Ceylon sapphires, synonymous with Sri Lanka, are prized for their vivid hues, ranging from deep blue to velvety cornflower blue. The unique combination of color intensity and clarity sets them apart.

Padparadscha Sapphire

"A rare and exquisite variety"

Padparadscha sapphires exhibit a mesmerizing blend of pink and orange hues, often described as "sunset orange." Their unique color, combined with their rarity, makes them highly valuable.

Star Ruby

"A celestial phenomenon in gemstone form"

Star rubies display a distinct asterism, creating a mesmerizing six-rayed star pattern that appears to float across the surface of the stone.

Cat's Eye Chrysoberyl

"A mesmerizing optical effect"

The distinctive chatoyancy effect creates a bright line that appears to glide across the surface of the stone, resembling a cat's eye.

Ceylon Diamond

"Rare and exceptional brilliance"

Though rare, Sri Lankan diamonds are known for their exceptional quality and unique characteristics. These natural diamonds showcase remarkable clarity and fire.

Alexandrite

"The color-changing miracle"

Alexandrite is famous for its remarkable ability to change color, appearing green in daylight and red in incandescent light. Sri Lankan alexandrite is particularly prized for its dramatic color change.

Ceylon Spinel

"The forgotten gem of royalty"

Ceylon spinels are celebrated for their vibrant colors and exceptional clarity. These gems have adorned royal crowns and continue to captivate with their brilliance.
