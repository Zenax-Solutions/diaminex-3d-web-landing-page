Ceylon Sapphire

"Renowned for their exceptional color saturation and clarity"

Characteristics

  • Exceptional color range from light to deep blue
  • Superior clarity and transparency
  • Excellent durability (9 on Mohs scale)
  • Natural fluorescence in some specimens

History and Significance

Ceylon sapphires have been prized for centuries, adorning royal jewelry and sacred artifacts. Their unique combination of color, clarity, and size has made them among the most sought-after gemstones in the world.

Mining and Origin

These precious gems are primarily found in Sri Lanka's gem-rich regions, particularly in Ratnapura, known as the "City of Gems." The traditional mining methods, passed down through generations, ensure sustainable extraction while preserving the environment.

Quality Factors

Color

The most valued Ceylon sapphires display a medium to medium-dark blue color with vivid saturation.

Clarity

Known for their exceptional clarity, with minimal inclusions visible to the naked eye.

Care and Maintenance

  • Clean with warm, soapy water and a soft brush
  • Avoid exposure to harsh chemicals
  • Store separately to prevent scratching
  • Regular professional cleaning recommended
