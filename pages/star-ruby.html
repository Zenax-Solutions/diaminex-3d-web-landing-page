Star Ruby

"A celestial phenomenon in gemstone form"

Characteristics

  • Distinctive six-rayed star effect (asterism)
  • Rich red color with varying intensity
  • High durability (9 on Mohs scale)
  • Natural phenomenon caused by rutile inclusions

History and Significance

Star rubies have been revered throughout history as powerful talismans and symbols of royalty. The star effect, known as asterism, has captivated collectors and enthusiasts for centuries.

Mining and Origin

Sri Lankan star rubies are particularly prized for their exceptional quality and well-defined stars. They are found in the island's ancient alluvial deposits.

Quality Factors

Star Quality

The best specimens display sharp, well-defined stars that are centered and visible from various angles.

Color

Deep red to purplish-red colors are most valued, with good transparency despite the presence of rutile needles.

Care and Maintenance

  • Clean with warm water and mild soap
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and ultrasonic cleaners
  • Store separately to prevent scratching
  • Protect from extreme temperature changes
