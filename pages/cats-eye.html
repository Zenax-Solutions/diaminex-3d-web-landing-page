Cat's Eye Chrysoberyl

"A mesmerizing optical effect"

Characteristics

  • Strong chatoyancy effect (cat's eye phenomenon)
  • Honey to greenish-yellow color
  • Excellent durability (8.5 on Mohs scale)
  • Sharp and well-defined eye effect

History and Significance

Cat's Eye Chrysoberyl has been highly valued throughout history, particularly in Sri Lanka where some of the finest specimens are found. The stone's unique optical effect has made it a favorite among collectors and connoisseurs.

Mining and Origin

Sri Lankan Cat's Eye Chrysoberyl is mined from alluvial deposits in the island's gem-bearing regions. The unique geological conditions in these areas contribute to the formation of high-quality specimens.

Quality Factors

Eye Effect

The most valuable specimens display a sharp, well-defined cat's eye effect that moves smoothly across the surface.

Color

Honey-colored to greenish-yellow stones are most prized, with good transparency and minimal inclusions.

Care and Maintenance

  • Clean with warm water and mild soap
  • Avoid ultrasonic and steam cleaners
  • Store separately from other gemstones
  • Protect from harsh chemicals and extreme temperatures
